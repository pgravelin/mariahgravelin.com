FILLER

Mariah Gravelin is a photographer and artist based in New York City. She grew up in a small town in Southeastern Connecticut and moved to New York at 17. Her interest in and passion for photography began at a young age with her best friend’s camera, and grew even more when she was passed down her grandmother’s old film camera (she still loves to shoot with it!) In 2018, she received her BFA from the Ailey School and Fordham University, where she also minored in Visual Arts, and got to explore and expand her portfolio amongst the dance community. She has photographed artists from the Alvin Ailey American Dance Theater, the Ailey School, New York City Ballet, Pennsylvania Ballet, Jessica Lang Dance, Buglisi Dance Theater, Graham II, and Peridance Capezio Dance Company. Her work has appeared in Pointe Magazine (2018) and Aspire Magazine (2017).

Mariah is also a dancer with the Limon Dance Company in NYC, and enjoys every moment she gets to spend with the dancers, artists, and the people she meets. Alongside dance photography, she has developed a great interest in portraiture and uses her photography as a medium to tell stories and capture the beauty she sees around her.
